## Glossary

###There are a few terms we use here that are common to the anime fandom or we just plain made them up. 
---

**BDF** - *Big Dumb Fun* 
A made up genre of anime that's just *big dumb fun*.

**PTW** - *Plan to watch* 
A list of shows we are planning to watch.. eventually.
